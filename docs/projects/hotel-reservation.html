LanIyaStar Hotel Reservation System was the final project for our Computer Programming 2 in the second semester of our freshman year.

Opportunities

Throught this project, I got in close contact with building a GUI-based Java application which was a great experience because I felt like the game just leveled-up. Because of the business rules, I was forced to come up and develop basic algorithms that challenged my critical thinking skills. Furthermore, developing the database was an eye opener on what I have to deal with in the field of data science.

Challenges

While it may be a great learning opportunity, it's wasn't easy getting the job done. The first challenge I faced was the interaction between the components in Java GUI. Additionally, coming up with the proper algorithms was the real wall for me as I am not equipped with a good base in mathematics nor a deeper understanding of programming which I needed. Lastly, learning how to utilize the database connectivity took me a while to grasp, but I'm happy it worked out in the end.
